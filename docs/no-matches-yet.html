🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

Why haven't I received any matches?

If you've submitted a journey but haven't heard anything yet, there are a few common reasons. Most are easily resolved.

The most common reasons

1. Not enough users in your area yet

Community Carpool matches people who live and work near each other. If there aren't yet many registered users commuting your route, the pool of potential matches is simply too small to return results within your preferred distance. The service is growing — matches may appear as more people register nearby.

2. Your matching distance preference is too narrow

When you submitted your journey, you chose a maximum matching distance (for example, 3 km or 5 km). If this is set very tight, fewer people will qualify as matches. Consider submitting a second journey with a wider distance preference to see if that returns results.

3. Your journey was submitted very recently

Matching runs periodically. If you submitted today, you may simply not have been through a full matching cycle yet. See How long does matching take? for timing details.

4. Match notifications may be in your spam folder

Our emails occasionally land in spam or promotions folders. Check those folders for emails from Community Carpool, then add our address to your contacts to prevent it in future. If you find it there, also see I'm not receiving emails.

5. You may have unsubscribed from match notifications

If you previously unsubscribed from match notification emails, you won't receive alerts even when matches are found. You can re-enable them via the preferences link in any email, or contact support if you no longer have an email to work from. See Managing email notifications.

💡
Your journey is still active — No match yet does not mean there is a problem with your submission. Your journey stays in the system and is included in every matching cycle until it expires after 90 days of no new matches.

What you can do

  • Wait — matches are found as new users register. This is normal, especially early in the service.
  • Submit a second journey with a wider matching distance.
  • Check your spam folder for missed match notification emails.
  • If you believe there is an issue with your submission, contact us via the support page.
