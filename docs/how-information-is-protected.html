🔒 Privacy & Your Data

How your information is protected

Community Carpool was built with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. This page explains the protections built into the platform to keep your information safe.

👤

Names are masked

Until a mutual match is confirmed, other users only see your initial followed by asterisks (e.g. "S****"). Your full name is never displayed to anyone who hasn't matched with you mutually.

📧

Email is hidden until mutual

Your email address is never shown to another user unless both of you have independently clicked Interested on the same match. One-sided interest reveals nothing.

📍

Approximate locations only

You enter a neighbourhood or community name, not an exact address. The matching algorithm works with area-level coordinates, not your front door.

🔑

Token-secured access

Your matches page is only accessible via a unique, time-limited secure link sent to your email. It cannot be guessed or accessed by anyone without your link.

Who can see your data?

  • Other users — Only see your masked name and general route until a mutual match. After mutual match: your first name and email only.
  • Community Carpool support staff — Can see your name, email, and journey details for the purpose of handling support requests. They cannot see match decisions or mutual match contacts.
  • Third parties — Your data is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers or marketers.

How your data is stored

All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. The platform is built on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security controls, meaning even application-level queries cannot access data outside permitted scopes.

💡
You are in control — You can request deletion of all your data at any time. See Deleting your account for the full process.
