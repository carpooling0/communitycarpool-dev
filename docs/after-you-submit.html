🚀 Getting Started

What happens after you submit

Once your journey is registered, the platform takes over. Here's exactly what happens behind the scenes and what you can expect in your inbox.

Immediately after submission

You will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes. This email confirms your journey is active and gives you a summary of what you entered. No action is needed on your part — keep an eye on your inbox. The confirmation email is informational — it confirms what you submitted but does not contain your matches link yet. Your matches link will arrive in a separate email once matches are found.

💡
Check your spam folder — Confirmation and match emails occasionally land in spam, especially the first time. Add noreply@communitycarpool.org to your contacts to prevent this.

How the matching algorithm works

The system periodically compares all active journeys and looks for overlapping routes. A match is created when two journeys meet all of the following criteria:

  • The pickup points (home areas) are within the maximum distance both users have set.
  • The drop-off points (workplaces) are also within the matching distance of each other.
  • Both journeys are currently active (not paused or expired).

When a match is found, both users receive an email notification with a secure link to view their matches.

The match notification email

The email subject will be "Your Carpool Update". It contains a personalised, secure link to your matches page. This link is unique to you — do not share it. It expires after 120 days; after that, you can request a new link via the support page.

ℹ️
How long until a match? This depends on how many people in your area have registered. In active schemes, most people receive their first match within a few days. In newer or smaller schemes it may take longer. See Why haven't I received any matches? for more detail.

Your journey expiry

Active journeys remain in the matching pool for 90 days. If no new matches are found within that window, your journey will be automatically deactivated to keep the pool fresh. You will receive a reminder email before expiry and can resubmit at any time.

What you can do while waiting

  • Nothing — the system works automatically in the background.
  • If your route changes, submit a new journey with the updated details.
  • If you change your mind entirely, you can request account deletion at any time.
