⚙️ Managing Your Journey

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account at any time. Deletion removes all of your personal data from our systems — your user record, all journey submissions, and all match decisions.

How to request deletion

1

Go to the deletion request page

Visit Community Carpool and scroll to the bottom, or navigate directly to the deletion request option via the site footer.

2

Enter your registered email address

Submit the email address you used to register. If the address is found in our system, a confirmation email will be sent to it.

3

Confirm via the email link

Open the confirmation email and click the deletion link. This verifies your identity and schedules your account for permanent deletion.

4

Deletion is processed overnight

Confirmed deletions are processed during the early hours of the following morning. You will receive a final confirmation email once your data has been removed.

⚠️
Deletion is permanent — Once processed, your account cannot be restored. All journey history, match decisions, and contact information are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your user record (name and email address)
  • All journey submissions associated with your account
  • All match decisions (Interested / Not for me) you have recorded
  • Any mutual match connections involving your account

What is not affected

  • Support tickets you submitted — these are retained for 1 year after resolution for our records, but are anonymised after your account is deleted.
  • Email event logs (open/click tracking) — these are anonymised and retained for 90 days per our standard retention policy.

Cancelling a deletion request

After clicking the confirmation link, your deletion is scheduled but not yet processed. If you change your mind, you can cancel the request before it is processed by contacting us via the support page. Include your email address and state that you want to cancel a pending deletion. Our team will cancel the request and your account will remain active.

ℹ️
Email not recognised? — If you submit an email address that is not in our system, no confirmation email is sent. This is intentional — it prevents account enumeration. If you are unsure which email you registered with, contact us via the support page.

Resubmitting after deletion

If you ever want to use Community Carpool again after your account has been deleted, simply fill in the registration form as a new user. There is no waiting period.
