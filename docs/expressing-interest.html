🔗 Your Matches

Expressing interest or declining

On your matches page you have two choices for each match — express interest or decline. Neither action is visible to the other person until a mutual match occurs.

The two buttons

1

Figure 1 — A pending match card with action buttons

  • 1The Interested and Not for me buttons appear on every pending match card. Your choice is recorded privately — the other person is not notified either way unless both of you choose Interested.

What "Interested" does

  • Your interest is recorded privately on our end.
  • The other person is not notified that you clicked Interested.
  • If they also click Interested independently, it becomes a mutual match — contact details are then revealed to both of you simultaneously. See What happens when it's mutual.
  • If they click "Not for me", the match is quietly removed from both your views — you are not informed of their choice.

What "Not for me" does

  • The match card is removed from your matches page.
  • The other person is not notified that you declined.
  • The match will not reappear for that journey pairing.
ℹ️
Privacy guarantee — Your decision is never shared with the other person unless it results in a mutual match. Declining a match does not send any notification to the other user.

Can I change my mind?

Once you click Not for me, the match is permanently dismissed for that journey pairing and cannot be undone through the app. If you believe you declined by mistake, contact support and a team member can review it.

If you clicked Interested and have not yet had a mutual match, contact support to reverse it.
