🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

How to contact support

Community Carpool is a volunteer-run service. Our team is small and we handle requests via a support ticket system. This page explains how to reach us and what to expect.

How to submit a support request

All support requests go through the support page on the Community Carpool website. There is no phone line or direct email address.

1

Go to the support page

Visit the support page from any browser.

2

Select the ticket type

Choose the category that best describes your issue. This helps us route it to the right person.

3

Enter your details and describe the issue

Include your registered email address. The more context you can provide, the faster we can help.

4

Submit and wait for a response

We aim to respond within one to two working days. You'll receive a reply to the email address you provided.

What type of request do you have?

🔧

Technical issue

Your matches link isn't working, you can't submit the form, pages are loading incorrectly, or something on the site appears broken. Include as much detail as possible — what you were trying to do, what happened, and which device/browser you were using.

⚠️

Report a user

You've had a concerning experience with a match — inappropriate contact, misuse of contact details, or any other conduct issue. Include the name or email of the person if known, and a description of what happened.

💬

General enquiry

Questions about how the service works, requests to update or deactivate a journey, data requests, or anything else not covered by the above categories.

⚠️
Account deletion requests are not handled through support tickets — To delete your account, use the dedicated deletion flow on the main site. See Deleting your account for full instructions.

What to include in your message

  • Your registered email address — this is how we identify your account.
  • A clear description of the problem or question.
  • Any relevant details: what you were trying to do, error messages you saw, or steps you've already tried.

Response times

We aim to respond to all tickets within one to two working days. Tickets submitted on weekends or public holidays will be addressed at the start of the next working day.

For data access requests (a copy of all data we hold about you), we will respond within 5 working days.
