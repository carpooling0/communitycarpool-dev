⚙️ Managing Your Journey

Managing email notifications

Community Carpool sends a small number of emails related to your journey. You can control which categories you receive using the preference links included in every email.

Types of emails we send

Email typeWhen it's sentCan be turned off?
Match notifications When new potential carpool matches are found for your journey Yes
Mutual match alerts When both you and a match have expressed interest — contact details are now revealed Yes (part of match notifications)
Journey reminders Periodic reminders if your journey has been inactive or is approaching expiry Yes
System emails Account deletion confirmations, support ticket responses, and security-related notices No — these are essential account communications

How to update your preferences

Every email from Community Carpool includes an Unsubscribe / Manage preferences link at the bottom. Clicking it opens your personal preferences page where you can toggle each category on or off.

1

Open any email from Community Carpool

Look for a match notification, reminder, or mutual match email in your inbox.

2

Click the unsubscribe link

At the bottom of any Community Carpool email, click the Manage email preferences or Unsubscribe link.

3

Adjust your settings

Toggle match notifications and reminders on or off. Changes take effect immediately.

ℹ️
The preference link is unique to your account — It does not require a password and is safe to use from your email client. It only updates preferences for the email address it was sent to.

What happens when you turn off match notifications

Your journey remains active and continues to be included in the matching process. You simply won't receive email alerts when new matches are found. You can still visit your matches page directly using any link from a previous email (as long as it is within its 120-day validity window) — your results will be up to date.

Re-enabling notifications

Open the preference page again (via any existing email link) and re-enable the categories you'd like to receive. There is no time limit — preferences can be changed at any time.

💡
No emails to work from? — If you can't find a previous email to access your preferences, contact us via the support page and we can reset your preferences for you.
