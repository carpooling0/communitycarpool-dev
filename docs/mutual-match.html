🔗 Your Matches

What happens when it's mutual

A mutual match happens when both you and your match independently click Interested. At that point, contact details are revealed to both parties and you can reach out directly.

What you'll see on your matches page

1

Figure 1 — A mutual match with full name and email revealed

  • 1The mutual match card shows a green "You're both interested!" banner, the full name of your match, and their email address. The Interested / Not for me buttons are replaced by the contact panel.

What gets revealed

  • Full name of your match (no longer masked).
  • Email address of your match.

Your full name and email are simultaneously revealed to them on their matches page.

💡
What to do next — Send a friendly email to introduce yourself, confirm your typical schedule (days, departure time), and discuss who will drive. There's no fixed format — just treat it like reaching out to a colleague.

Email notification

You will also receive an email notification when a mutual match is confirmed. The email will tell you that contact details are now available on your matches page. You'll need to open the matches page via the link to see the full details.

After you connect

Community Carpool's role ends here — the carpool arrangement is entirely between you and your match. You can agree on timing, driving rotation, cost-sharing (if any), and whatever else works for both of you.

What if it doesn't work out?

If after speaking with your match you decide the carpool isn't right for you, that's completely fine. You don't need to do anything in the app — just keep your journey active and you may be matched with others. If you'd like to flag an issue with a match, use the support page.
