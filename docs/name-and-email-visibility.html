🔒 Privacy & Your Data

When your name & email are visible

Your personal details are revealed progressively depending on where you are in the matching process. This table gives a clear breakdown.

Visibility by stage

StageYour name visible to match?Your email visible to match?
Journey submitted, no match yet No No
Match found, neither party has responded Masked only (e.g. "S****") No
You clicked Interested, they have not yet Masked only No
They clicked Interested, you have not yet Masked only No
Mutual match — both clicked Interested Yes — full first name Yes — full email address
ℹ️
One-sided interest is never disclosed — If you click Interested but your match clicks "Not for me", they are not told that you were interested. The match simply disappears from both views.

How names are masked

The masking format is: first letter of your first name followed by four asterisks. For example:

  • "Sarah" → S****
  • "David" → D****
  • "Alex" → A****

This gives just enough information to feel like a real person without revealing your identity prematurely.

After a mutual match

Once mutual, both parties see each other's first name and email address. Last names, phone numbers, and exact home addresses are never shared through the platform at any stage.
