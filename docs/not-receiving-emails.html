🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

I'm not receiving emails

If you've submitted a journey but aren't getting match notifications or other emails, work through the checklist below before contacting support.

Step-by-step checklist

1. Check your spam or junk folder

Emails from Community Carpool are occasionally filtered as spam, particularly for corporate or government email accounts with strict filtering. Look for emails from noreply@communitycarpool.org in your spam, junk, or promotions folders.

If you find them there, mark the email as Not Spam and add the sender address to your contacts. This teaches your email client to deliver future emails to your inbox.

2. Check you used the correct email address

Emails are sent to the address you entered when submitting your journey. If you have multiple email addresses, check that you're looking in the right inbox. A typo in the email field at registration is a common cause — for example, a missing letter or wrong domain.

If you suspect a typo, submit a support request and we can verify what address is on file. See How to contact support.

3. Check whether you're unsubscribed

If you previously clicked an unsubscribe link, match notifications will no longer be sent. Check your email preferences — if you have any previous email from Community Carpool, click the preferences link at the bottom to review and re-enable notifications. See Managing email notifications.

4. Check whether your journey has already expired

Journeys expire after 90 days of no new matches. Once expired, no further match emails are sent. If your journey has expired, resubmit from the homepage.

5. Wait — you may simply not have a match yet

If your journey is recent and your route doesn't yet have many registered users, no match notifications will be sent because no matches have been found. See Why haven't I received any matches? for more context.

ℹ️
Corporate email accounts — If your email is hosted by a large organisation (employer, government body, university), their IT security filters may be blocking our emails entirely. Try registering with a personal email address instead, or ask your IT helpdesk to whitelist our sending domain.

Still not receiving emails after all of the above?

Contact us via the support page. Include the email address you registered with and a brief description of what you're experiencing. We can check whether emails are being sent successfully from our end and advise on next steps.
