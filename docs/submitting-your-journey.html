🚀 Getting Started

Submitting your journey

Registering your commute takes less than a minute. This page walks through every field on the form and explains what each one is used for.

Step 1 — Your Details

Open the Community Carpool homepage and fill in your details in the first section.

1
2

Figure 1 — The Your Details section

  • 1First Name — Used to personalise your notification emails and displayed (partially masked) to potential matches.
  • 2Email — Where match notifications are sent. Only revealed to another user after a mutual match. Use your work email if this is a workplace scheme.

Step 2 — From & To locations

Type your home area or community name in the From field and your workplace in the To field. As suggestions appear in a dropdown below the field, tap or click one of them to select it — the form will not accept a location unless it has been chosen from the dropdown list.

1
2
3
4

Figure 2 — From, To, and Matching Distance fields

  • 1From field — Enter your home area (e.g. "JVC District 11") or a nearby landmark. Do not enter your exact apartment number or villa address.
  • 2Privacy notice — A reminder not to include specific building or villa numbers to protect your exact address.
  • 3To field — Your workplace or destination area (e.g. "DIFC Gate Village").
  • 4Matching Distance — The maximum distance a match's pickup or drop-off point can be from yours. Default is 3–5 km. Increase this if you are in a less populated area.
⚠️
Use areas, not exact addresses — Enter a neighbourhood, community name, or workplace building — not your apartment number or villa address. This protects your precise location while still enabling accurate matching.

Step 3 — Matching Distance slider

The slider has four positions:

  • 1–3 km — Only very close matches. Best in high-density areas.
  • 3–5 km — The recommended default for most areas.
  • 5–8 km — Wider net. Useful in suburban or spread-out areas.
  • 8–10 km — Maximum range. Good for areas with fewer users.

You can always resubmit with a different distance preference if your initial setting returns no matches.

Step 4 — Agree and submit

1
2

Figure 3 — Agreeing to terms and submitting

  • 1Terms & Conditions checkbox — Read the terms before ticking. The button stays greyed out until this is checked.
  • 2Register My Journey — Submits your journey. You will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes.
ℹ️
Confirmation email — After submitting, check your inbox for a confirmation email from Community Carpool. If it doesn't arrive within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. The email confirms your journey is registered and explains what happens next.

Common submission errors

  • "Please select a location from the suggestions" — You must pick an option from the Google Maps autocomplete dropdown, not free-type the address.
  • "Email already registered" — Your email is linked to an existing journey. You can submit a second journey using the same email.
