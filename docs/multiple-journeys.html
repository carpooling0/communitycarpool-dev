⚙️ Managing Your Journey

Submitting multiple journeys

You can register more than one journey under the same email address. Each journey is matched independently and appears as its own section on your matches page.

When would I need multiple journeys?

  • You commute from different locations on different days (e.g. you stay with family in another area mid-week).
  • You work across two different offices or sites and want matches for each.
  • Your circumstances changed since your original submission — for example, you moved home — and you want to keep the old journey while also registering the new one.
  • You want to try a different matching distance to see if it returns more results.

How to submit a second journey

  1. Go to the Community Carpool homepage.
  2. Fill in the form with your updated or alternative route details.
  3. Use the same email address as your first submission — the system will link both journeys to your account.
  4. Submit as normal.
ℹ️
All journeys appear on one page — When you open your matches page via any notification email, you'll see all your registered journeys and their respective matches grouped together on a single page.

Is there a limit?

You can have up to 10 active journeys at a time. If you reach this limit and try to submit another, the form will let you know — archive one of your existing journeys first to free up a slot. Please register only journeys you genuinely intend to make — unnecessary submissions reduce the quality of the pool for everyone.
