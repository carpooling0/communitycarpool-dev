🔗 Your Matches

Accessing your matches page

Your matches page is a personalised, secure page that shows all the carpool matches found for your registered journeys. You can only reach it via the link in your match notification email.

How to open your matches page

  1. Open the "Your Carpool Update" email from Community Carpool in your inbox.
  2. Click the "View My Matches" button or link inside the email.
  3. Your matches page will open in your browser, showing all current matches.
⚠️
The page cannot be accessed directly — navigating to the matches URL without a valid token will show an error screen. Always open it from your notification email. If you have lost the email, see My matches link isn't working.

Multiple journeys

If you have submitted more than one journey, each one appears as a separate section on the page with its own list of matches. Scroll down to see all of them.

Link expiry

The secure link in your email is valid for 120 days. After that, it will show a "link expired" message. If this happens, submit a support request and a new link will be issued.
