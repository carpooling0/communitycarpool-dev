🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

How long does matching take?

There is no fixed timeline for finding a match — it depends on how many people in your area are registered. Here's what to expect at each stage.

The matching process

When you submit a journey, you enter the matching pool. The system periodically runs a matching algorithm across all active submissions to find compatible pairs. When it finds a match above the minimum strength threshold, it sends you a notification email.

Time from submission to first match

For most users in active areas, a first match notification arrives within a few days. In less-populated areas, or for routes with fewer registered commuters, it may take longer — or no match may be found during the 30-day active window.

ℹ️
Matching depends on the pool size — The system can only match you with people who are already registered. If very few people commute your specific route, the wait may be longer regardless of how the system is configured.

Journey expiry

An active journey expires after 90 days following your last match notification. If a new match is found within that window, the 90-day timer resets. If no match is found and the journey expires, you will receive an expiry notification and can resubmit at any time.

Time from match to mutual match

Once you and another person have both been notified of each other, a mutual match happens when both parties independently click Interested. This is entirely in the hands of both users — there's no system timeout. Some mutual matches happen the same day; others may take a week or more depending on when each person checks their matches page.

Tips to get matched sooner

  • Widen your matching distance — Submit a second journey with a larger radius (e.g. 5 km or 8 km instead of 3 km).
  • Check your spam folder — Notifications may be arriving but landing in spam. See I'm not receiving emails.
  • Tell colleagues — If more people from your workplace or neighbourhood register, your chances of a match improve significantly.
💡
New users keep joining — Even if you haven't had a match yet, a compatible person could register tomorrow. Your journey stays active for 90 days and is checked in every matching cycle.
