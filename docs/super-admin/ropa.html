Record of Processing Activities (ROPA)

Required under GDPR Article 30. This document records every activity in which Community Carpool processes personal data — what data, why, where it goes, how long it's kept. Review and update whenever a new data flow is introduced.

Controller details

Organisation nameCommunity Carpool
Websitecommunitycarpool.org
Contact emailsupport@communitycarpool.org
Data Protection OfficerNot appointed (not required at current scale and data categories)
Last reviewedApril 2026
📌
When to update this document: any time you add a new form field, integrate a new third-party service, change retention periods, or introduce a new use of existing data. Treat it as a living document.
1
Journey registration
FieldDetail
PurposeRegistering a user's commute journey so they can be matched with compatible carpoolers
Legal basisConsent — user explicitly accepts Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy at submission (terms checkbox, version recorded)
Data subjectsRegistered users (adults 16+)
Personal data collected First name · Email address · Home/work location addresses (text) · Home/work coordinates (lat/lng) · IP address · Country (derived from IP) · Terms version accepted · Timestamp
Special category dataNone
SourceDirectly from the data subject via the web form
RecipientsInternal team (via admin dashboard) only. No data sold or shared externally.
Third-party processors Supabase (database hosting, Singapore) — stores all user and submission data
Google Maps API (address autocomplete) — receives address search strings; no name or email
Mapbox (road distance) — receives lat/lng pairs only; no name or email
ipwho.is (IP geolocation) — receives IP address, returns country code; no data retained by us beyond country code
International transfersSupabase hosted in Singapore (ap-southeast-1). Covered by Supabase's standard terms which incorporate appropriate safeguards. Google Maps and Mapbox servers in the US — covered by their standard DPA/SCCs.
Retention periodJourney data retained while account is active. Deleted within 30 days of a confirmed account deletion request. Expired journeys archived (status = expired) but not purged automatically — manual review or cron job required.
Automated decisionsMatch scoring is automated (algorithm-based, no human review). Not used for decisions with legal or similarly significant effects — users are free to ignore any match.
2
Carpool matching & match notifications
FieldDetail
PurposeComparing journey data across users to identify compatible matches and notifying users by email when a match is found
Legal basisConsent (as above — integral to the service the user signed up for)
Data subjectsRegistered users with active journeys
Personal data usedJourney coordinates (lat/lng), from/to location addresses, distance preference, name (first name only, masked as "A**** B." in match previews), email (only revealed on mutual interest)
Special category dataNone
RecipientsMatched users receive each other's masked name until both express mutual interest, at which point full name and email are revealed to both parties only
Third-party processors Resend (email delivery, US) — receives recipient email address, first name, and match notification content (masked names, match link). No coordinates or full match details in email body.
International transfersResend processes emails in the US. Covered by Resend's standard DPA and SCCs.
Retention periodMatch records retained while either linked journey is active. Deleted with account on deletion request.
Automated decisionsMatch score calculated automatically. No human review. Not legally significant — purely informational.
3
Email preference management (unsubscribe)
FieldDetail
PurposeAllowing users to opt out of match notifications, reminders, and/or marketing emails
Legal basisLegal obligation (right to withdraw consent / opt out of marketing under GDPR and PECR)
Data subjectsRegistered users
Personal data usedEmail address, unsubscribe preference flags (unsubscribed_matches, unsubscribed_reminders, unsubscribed_marketing)
Special category dataNone
RecipientsInternal only
Third-party processorsNone beyond Supabase (storage)
International transfersSupabase (Singapore) — as above
Retention periodRetained until account is deleted
Automated decisionsNone
4
Support ticket submission
FieldDetail
PurposeReceiving and responding to user support requests, including technical issues and reports of other users
Legal basisLegitimate interest (providing customer support; resolving safety/moderation issues)
Data subjectsAny person who submits a support request; may also involve third parties named in the ticket
Personal data collectedName, email address, free-text message, ticket type, submission timestamp. May contain third-party personal data if the user describes another user.
Special category dataPotentially — if a user describes a sensitive situation involving another person. Handled with discretion.
RecipientsInternal support team via admin dashboard. Notification email sent to carpooling0@gmail.com on new ticket.
Third-party processors Resend — sends notification of new ticket to support inbox
Google (Gmail) — support notification received at carpooling0@gmail.com
International transfersResend (US); Google (US). Both covered by standard DPA/SCCs.
Retention periodTickets retained indefinitely for moderation history. No automated deletion. Consider periodic manual review.
Automated decisionsNone
5
Account deletion
FieldDetail
PurposeProcessing user requests to permanently delete their account and all associated personal data
Legal basisLegal obligation (GDPR right to erasure, Article 17)
Data subjectsRegistered users requesting deletion
Personal data usedEmail address (to verify request via token), deletion request timestamp, deletion log record
Special category dataNone
RecipientsInternal admin team (processes deletion). Resend (sends confirmation email to user).
Third-party processorsResend — sends deletion confirmation email
International transfersResend (US) — as above
Retention periodAll personal data deleted within 30 days of confirmed deletion request. A deletion_log record (email + timestamp only) is retained for audit purposes — this is a legitimate interest in proving compliance.
Automated decisionsNone — deletion is triggered by cron job but verified by admin before permanent execution (Delete Now button requires PIN)
6
Campaign attribution & referral tracking
FieldDetail
PurposeUnderstanding which marketing campaigns or referral links led users to register, to measure campaign effectiveness
Legal basisLegitimate interest (measuring campaign ROI; no profiling or targeting beyond attribution)
Data subjectsRegistered users who arrived via a referral link or UTM-tagged URL
Personal data usedReferral code (ref_code), UTM source/medium/campaign tags, linked to user record. No third-party tracking pixels or cookies involved — attribution is first-party only.
Special category dataNone
RecipientsInternal team via admin dashboard only
Third-party processorsNone — attribution is stored in Supabase only (no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel)
International transfersSupabase (Singapore) — as above
Retention periodRetained with user record; deleted on account deletion
Automated decisionsNone
7
Admin access & audit logging
FieldDetail
PurposeControlling internal access to the platform and maintaining an audit trail of administrative actions
Legal basisLegitimate interest (security, accountability, compliance)
Data subjectsAdmin team members; indirectly, platform users whose records are viewed or modified
Personal data usedAdmin: name, email, password hash (PBKDF2), role, IP address, last login timestamp. Audit log: admin name, action taken, entity email affected, IP address, timestamp.
Special category dataNone
RecipientsSuper-Admin only (audit log access)
Third-party processorsSupabase (storage)
International transfersSupabase (Singapore) — as above
Retention periodAdmin session records expire after 8 hours. Audit log retained indefinitely for accountability. Admin user records retained while account is active; deactivated on offboarding.
Automated decisionsNone
