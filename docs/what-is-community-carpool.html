🚀 Getting Started

What is Community Carpool?

Community Carpool is a free, privacy-first platform that matches colleagues or neighbours on similar commute routes so they can share the drive — no app to install, no account to create.

How it works in a nutshell

You submit your home and work locations. Our algorithm compares your route against everyone else who has registered and calculates how closely your journeys overlap. When a good match is found, you receive an email notification with a secure link to view and respond to your matches.

1

Register your journey

Fill in your name, work email, home area, and workplace. Takes under a minute.

2

We find your matches

The system runs matching automatically. You get an email when someone on a similar route is found.

3

Express interest — then connect

If both you and your match click Interested, contact details are revealed and you can coordinate directly.

What makes it different?

  • No app required — everything happens via email links and a mobile-friendly web page.
  • Privacy by design — your full name and email are never shown to another user unless there is a mutual match.
  • Completely free — there are no subscription fees or in-app purchases.
  • No commitment — you can deactivate your journey or delete your account at any time.
💡
Who runs it? Community Carpool is operated by your organisation or community group. If you have questions about who manages it, reach out via the support page.

What Community Carpool is not

Community Carpool is not a ride-hailing service like Uber or Careem. There is no payment system and no driver ratings. It simply connects people who are already making the same journey so they can choose to share it — the arrangement is entirely between you and your match.
