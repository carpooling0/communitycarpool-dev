Metric Definitions
Visitors — Unique individuals identified by browser fingerprint (cookie + IP + user-agent hash) within the selected period. A visitor returning across multiple days counts once per day in daily rollups but once total in a range query.
Views (Pageviews) — Total number of individual page loads. One visitor browsing 5 pages = 5 views. Views ÷ Visitors gives average pages per visitor.
Visits (Sessions) — A continuous browsing session. A new session starts when the same visitor returns after 30+ minutes of inactivity. One visitor can have multiple visits. Views ÷ Visits = pages per session.
Bounce Rate — % of visits that ended after a single page with no further interaction. 60% means 6 in 10 sessions saw only one page. Lower = better engagement. Umami counts a bounce as: one page load, no further events recorded before tab close.
Source: Umami Analytics. Data refreshes on each panel open (Live tab) or on manual sync (Archive tab).
