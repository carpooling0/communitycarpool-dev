Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 April 2026  ·  Version 1.0

The short version

We collect only what's needed to match you with carpool partners — your name, email, and commute route. We never sell your data. Your contact details are only shared with another user after both of you express mutual interest. You can delete your account at any time.

1. Who we are

Community Carpool ("we", "us", "our") operates the carpooling platform at communitycarpool.org. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at: carpooling0@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

Data Why we collect it Lawful basis
First name To personalise communications and partially display to potential matches Contract performance
Email address To send match notifications and account communications Contract performance
From / To locations To run the matching algorithm and find compatible carpool partners Contract performance
Matching distance preference To filter matches by how far from your route a partner can be Contract performance

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or sensitive personal data.

3. How we use your data

  • Matching — Your route data is used to identify other users with similar commutes. We use geographic distance calculations to determine match strength.
  • Match notifications — We email you when a new match is found for your journey.
  • Contact reveal — Your full name and email address are only shared with another user after both of you independently express interest in each other. Neither party sees the other's contact details until this mutual interest is confirmed.
  • Service communications — We may email you about your account, changes to the platform, or this Privacy Policy.
  • Platform improvement — Anonymised usage data helps us understand how the platform is used and improve it.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

5. Cookies

You can decline analytics cookies via the cookie banner shown on your first visit. Declining analytics does not affect any platform functionality.

6. How long we keep your data

Data Retention period
Your submission and profile While your journey is active. Deactivated journeys are retained for 90 days then purged.
Match records Retained while either user's journey is active, then purged with the journey.
After a deletion request Permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.
Email preference records Retained to honour unsubscribe requests, even after account deletion.
Analytics data (GA4) Retained by Google for 14 months (Google's default).

7. Your rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

✓ Right to access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

✓ Right to erasure

Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

✓ Right to rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate data we hold about you.

✓ Right to portability

Request your data in a machine-readable format.

✓ Right to object

Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

✓ Withdraw consent

Change your cookie preferences at any time via the banner.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at carpooling0@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

To delete your account directly, use the account deletion option on our platform — no email needed.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS
  • Database access is restricted — no direct public access to any table
  • API keys and secrets are stored as encrypted environment variables, never in code
  • Contact details are only revealed after explicit mutual consent between both users

No system is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Children

Community Carpool is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data through our platform, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. If changes are significant, we will notify registered users by email. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Previous versions are available on request.
