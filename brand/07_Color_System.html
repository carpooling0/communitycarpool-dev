Primary Color Usage

Dark Forest Green (#1B5C3A)

Purpose: Trust, stability, growth
Used for: Left pin, "CARPOOL" text, headings
When to use: Conveys security and environmental responsibility

Bright Chartreuse (#B4E035)

Purpose: Energy, sustainability, action
Used for: Right pin, "COMMUNITY" text, highlights
When to use: Draws attention, shows movement and energy

Button Green (#10B981)

Purpose: Call-to-action, interaction
Used for: Buttons, CTAs, links
When to use: Guides user interaction
