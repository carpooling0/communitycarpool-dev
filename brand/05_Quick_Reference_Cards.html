📋 Channel Voice Quick Reference

1-Page Cheat Sheets for All 8 Channels

🌐 WEBSITE

Website & Landing Pages

Tone
Warm, confident, clear
Key Metrics
• Formality: 50/100
• Seriousness: 40/100
• Frequency: Always available
✅ DoFocus on human benefit. Use "you" language.
❌ Don'tUse corporate jargon. Make unsupported claims.
Example
"Share your commute. Save money. Reduce carbon."
🔴 REDDIT

Reddit & Communities

Tone
Conversational, honest, like a peer
Key Metrics
• Formality: 20/100
• Seriousness: 40/100
• Frequency: Respond to comments
✅ DoAnswer questions genuinely. Be helpful.
❌ Don'tPost promotional content. Sound like a corporation.
Communities
r/bangalore, r/mumbai, r/delhi, r/sustainability
💼 LINKEDIN

LinkedIn (Professional)

Tone
Thoughtful, impact-focused, professional
Key Metrics
• Formality: 75/100
• Seriousness: 70/100
• Frequency: 1-2x per week
✅ DoShow business value. Share impact data.
❌ Don'tSound like a startup cliché. Use hype.
Best Times
Tue-Thu, 9-11am
🐦 TWITTER

Twitter/X (Sharp & Punchy)

Tone
Witty, sharp, conversational
Key Metrics
• Formality: 25/100
• Seriousness: 35/100
• Frequency: 3-5x per week
✅ DoReact to news. Share quick wins. Engage.
❌ Don'tUse corporate speak. Make unfounded claims.
Max Length
280 characters. Punchy beats perfect.
📧 EMAIL

Email & Newsletters

Tone
Warm, personal, like a trusted friend
Key Metrics
• Formality: 40/100
• Seriousness: 50/100
• Frequency: 1x per week
✅ DoLead with value. Use contractions. Be conversational.
❌ Don'tSend too often. Use excessive exclamation points.
Subject Line
Benefit + Curiosity + Personal
📝 BLOG

Blog & Long-Form Content

Tone
Educational, narrative, authentic
Key Metrics
• Formality: 60/100
• Seriousness: 65/100
• Frequency: 2x per month
✅ DoTell stories. Use data. Show original research.
❌ Don'tWrite thinly veiled marketing. Promote aggressively.
Ideal Length
1000-2000 words. Scannable with headers.
📱 IN-APP

In-App UI Copy

Tone
Clear, actionable, encouraging
Key Metrics
• Formality: 35/100
• Seriousness: 45/100
• Frequency: Contextual
✅ DoFocus on benefit. Use action verbs. Keep it short.
❌ Don'tUse jargon. Overexplain. Use corporate words.
Button Example
"Share your route" not "Submit transportation data"
🎯 CORPORATE PITCH

Corporate & School Pitches

Tone
Professional, data-backed, collaborative
Key Metrics
• Formality: 90/100
• Seriousness: 85/100
• Frequency: As scheduled
✅ DoShow ROI. Address pain points. Lead with their goal.
❌ Don'tOversell. Make unsupported claims. Ignore objections.
Key Data
56.5% match rate | 0 cost | Free to deploy
