Voice Consistency Checklist

Use this before publishing anything on any channel:

Matches brand essence: "We can do this. Together."
No corporate jargon or marketing hype
Conversational tone (contractions, simple words)
Second-person language ("you" not "users")
Benefit stated before features
All 4 emotions present: Empowered, Trusted, Relieved, Connected
Active voice (90%+ of the time)
Appropriate formality for channel
Authentic to the Optimistic Neighbor character
Would you say this to a neighbor? If no, rewrite it.
